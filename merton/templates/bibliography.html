Bibliography of the sources which Merton consulted during the writing of Journal #10, "The Red Diary," which have been conclusively identified.

  • A Friend of God (14th Century). The Book of the Poor in Spirit . Edited by C.F. Kelley. New York, Harper & Brothers, 1954.
  • Bars, Henry . Maritain en Notre Temps . Paris, Editions Bernard Grasset, 1959.
  • Basetti-Sani, Giulio . Mohammed et Saint Francois . Ottawa, Commissariat De TerreSainte, 1959.
  • Frankl, Victor E. From Death Camp to Existentialism . Boston, Beacon Press, 1959.
  • Heschel, Abraham J. Between God and Man . Edited by Fritz A. Rothschild. Toronto, Collier Macmillan, 1959.
  • Kadloubovsky, E. and Palmer, G.E.H., translators. Early Fathers from the Philokalia . London, Faber & Faber, Ltd., 1953.
  • Scholem, Gershom G. Major Trends in Jewish Mysticism . New York, Schocken Books, Inc., 1946.
  • Sofrony, Archimandrite . The Undistorted Image . Translated by Rosemary Edmunds. London, The Faith Press, 1958.
